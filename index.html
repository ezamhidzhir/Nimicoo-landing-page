Nimicoo Story

Ayam Gunting Gemok
Tanpa Tulang

Welcome to Nimicoo, the home of the ultimate Ayam Gunting. Our journey began in Kamunting, Perak, fueled by an obsession with crafting the perfect fried chicken cutlet. We believe that great street food shouldn't just taste good — it should feel rewarding.

That's why we take immense pride in our Ayam Gunting Gemok Tanpa Tulang, serving 100% boneless, premium chicken that is carefully marinated, deep-fried to a golden crisp, and packed with our signature flavors. Whether you've been supporting us since day one or you're discovering us for the first time, we are committed to serving you joy and maximum satisfaction in every single bite.

FAQs

Do you do dine in?

Yes! You are welcome to dine in at our outlet. We have comfortable seating available. Please visit us during our operating hours.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery via GrabFood and FoodPanda. You can also order directly via WhatsApp for self-pickup.

Is your chicken halal certified?

Yes, all our chicken is 100% halal. We only source from certified suppliers that fully comply with halal standards.

Can I place a bulk order for events?

Absolutely! We cater for open houses, kenduri, and corporate events. Please contact us at least 3 days in advance for bulk orders.
